## 🔍 **Key Concepts Explained**

### **Curse of Dimensionality**
- **Explained in**: TECHNICAL_JUSTIFICATION.md, Section 4
- **Summary**: High-dimensional spaces make distances meaningless
- **Impact**: KNN fails with 76,800-d face images
- **CNN Solution**: Reduces to 512-d embeddings

---

### **Feature Learning**
- **Explained in**: TECHNICAL_JUSTIFICATION.md, Section 3
- **Summary**: CNN learns features automatically, KNN uses hand-crafted
- **Impact**: CNN features are optimized, KNN features are not
- **Result**: CNN 99% vs KNN 75% accuracy

---

### **Algorithm Complexity**
- **Explained in**: TECHNICAL_JUSTIFICATION.md, Section 2
- **Summary**: CNN is O(1), KNN is O(n)
- **Impact**: CNN stays fast, KNN gets slower with more data
- **Result**: CNN 300× faster with 1000 students

---

### **Transfer Learning**
- **Explained in**: MODEL_COMPARISON.md
- **Summary**: Use pre-trained models without training
- **Impact**: 0 training time, immediate high accuracy
- **Result**: Production-ready from day 1
